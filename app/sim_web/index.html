RIS Propagation Models (LaTeX)

          

            This page consolidates the math used in this project and the two RIS propagation models used by
            Sionna v0.19.2.
          

        


        

          
Project Baseline Model (Machado/Tang-style Discrete Aperture Sum)

          
Used as the analytical foundation for RIS Lab validation and link-budget reasoning.

          
B1. Received Power (Discrete RIS Sum)

          

\[
P_r=\frac{P_tG_tG_rl_u^4}{16\pi^2}
\left|
\sum_{m=1}^{M}\sum_{n=1}^{N}
\frac{F_{n,m}^{\mathrm{combine}}\rho_{n,m}}{r^t_{n,m}r^r_{n,m}}
e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}(r^t_{n,m}+r^r_{n,m})}
\right|^2
\]
          

          
B2. Element-Wise Distance Definitions

          

\[
r^t_{n,m}=\sqrt{(x_t-x_{n,m})^2+(y_t-y_{n,m})^2+z_t^2},\qquad
r^r_{n,m}=\sqrt{(x_r-x_{n,m})^2+(y_r-y_{n,m})^2+z_r^2}
\]
          

          
B3. Refined Area-Based Form

          

\[
P_r=P_t\frac{G_tG_r(d_xd_y)^2}{16\pi^2}
\left|
\sum_{m=1}^{M}\sum_{n=1}^{N}
\frac{\sqrt{F_{n,m}^{\mathrm{combine}}}\Gamma_{n,m}}{r^t_{n,m}r^r_{n,m}}
e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{\lambda}(r^t_{n,m}+r^r_{n,m})}
\right|^2
\]
          

        


        

          
Sionna v0.19.2 RIS Model 1: compute_paths() (Degli-Esposti et al.)

          
Reradiation is modeled by equivalent local sources per RIS cell (RIS primer Eq. 44).

          
S1.1 Equivalent Reradiated Field

          

\[
\mathbf{E}_n(\mathbf{s})=
\sqrt{\frac{4\pi}{\lambda^2}\frac{P_nG_e(\mathbf{s})}{4\pi}}
\;e^{-j\mathbf{k}_r(\mathbf{s})^\mathsf{T}\mathbf{s}}
\]
          

          
S1.2 Cell Power

          

\[
P_n=\frac{\lambda^2}{64\pi^3}
\frac{P_tG_t(\hat{\mathbf{r}}_i)G_e(-\hat{\mathbf{r}}_i)}{r_i^2}
\]
          

          
S1.3 Reflection-Vector Law

          

\[
\mathbf{k}_r(\mathbf{s})=\mathbf{k}_i(\mathbf{s})+\nabla\chi(\mathbf{s})
\]
          

        


        

          
Sionna v0.19.2 RIS Model 2: coverage_map() (Vitucci et al.)

          
Uses a locally curved phase profile and angular taper for coverage-map computation (RIS primer Eq. 45–51).

          
S2.1 Local Reflection-Vector Law

          

\[
\mathbf{k}_r=\mathbf{k}_i+\nabla\chi(\mathbf{s})
\]
          

          
S2.2 Corrected Local Phase

          

\[
\tilde{\chi}(\mathbf{s})=\chi(\mathbf{s})-\frac{k}{2}(\mathbf{s}-\mathbf{c})^\mathsf{T}\mathbf{C}(\mathbf{s}-\mathbf{c})
\]
          

          
S2.3 Curvature Matrix and Geometric Relation

          

\[
\mathbf{C}=
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{\cos\theta_r^\*}{\lVert \mathbf{r}-\mathbf{c}\rVert} & 0\\
0 & \frac{1}{\lVert \mathbf{r}-\mathbf{c}\rVert}
\end{bmatrix},
\qquad
\mathbf{r}=\mathbf{c}+\hat{\mathbf{r}}_n\sqrt{\frac{A}{4\pi\cos\theta_r^\*}}
\]
          

          
S2.4 Received Power from Element Contribution

          

\[
P_r=\frac{P_nG_r\lambda^2}{(4\pi)^2r_r^2}
\left|\frac{1+\cos\theta_r^\*}{2}\right|^{2\alpha_r}
\]
          

          
S2.5 Coverage-Map Closed Form

          

\[
P_r=\frac{A^2}{\lambda^2(4\pi)^2r_i^2r_r^2}
\left|\frac{1+\cos\theta_r^\*}{2}\right|^{2\alpha_r}
P_tG_tG_r
\]
          

          
S2.6 Definition of \(P_n\)

          

\[
\text{with}\quad
P_n=\frac{A}{4\pi r_i^2\lambda^2}P_tG_t
\left|\frac{1+\cos\theta_r^\*}{2}\right|^{2\alpha_r}
\]
          

        

      


      

        
Symbol Legend

        

          
\(P_t\)
Transmit power.

          
\(P_r\)
Received power.

          
\(P_n\)
Power associated with RIS element \(n\) (or local reradiated contribution).

          
\(G_t, G_r\)
Transmit and receive antenna gains.

          
\(G_e(\cdot)\)
RIS unit-cell radiation gain pattern.

          
\(F_{n,m}^{\mathrm{combine}}\)
Joint normalized power radiation factor for the \((n,m)\)-th RIS cell.

          
\(\rho_{n,m}\)
Complex reflection coefficient in the project baseline equation.

          
\(\Gamma_{n,m}\)
Complex reflection coefficient in the refined Tang model notation.

          
\(\lambda\)
Wavelength.

          
\(l_u\)
Unit-cell side length (project baseline model).

          
\(d_x, d_y\)
RIS element pitch/size terms along x and y.

          
\(A\)
RIS unit-cell effective area.

          
\(M, N\)
Number of RIS cells along each axis.

          
\(m, n\)
RIS cell indices.

          
\(r^t_{n,m}\)
Distance from Tx to RIS cell \((n,m)\).

          
\(r^r_{n,m}\)
Distance from RIS cell \((n,m)\) to Rx.

          
\(r_i, r_r\)
Incident and reradiated path lengths used in Sionna formulas.

          
\((x_t,y_t,z_t)\)
Transmitter coordinates.

          
\((x_r,y_r,z_r)\)
Receiver coordinates.

          
\((x_{n,m},y_{n,m})\)
RIS cell \((n,m)\) coordinates in the RIS plane.

          
\(\mathbf{E}_n(\mathbf{s})\)
Equivalent reradiated electric field from unit \(n\) at point \(\mathbf{s}\).

          
\(\mathbf{s}\)
Point on the RIS surface (local surface coordinate).

          
\(\chi(\mathbf{s}),\tilde{\chi}(\mathbf{s})\)
RIS phase profile and locally corrected phase profile.

          
\(\mathbf{k}_i,\mathbf{k}_r\)
Incident and reflected wave vectors.

          
\(\nabla\chi(\mathbf{s})\)
Spatial phase gradient on the RIS.

          
\(k\)
Wavenumber (\(k=2\pi/\lambda\)).

          
\(\mathbf{C}\)
Local phase-curvature matrix used by the coverage-map model.

          
\(\lVert\cdot\rVert\)
Euclidean norm (vector magnitude).

          
\((\cdot)^\mathsf{T}\)
Vector/matrix transpose.

          
\(\mathbf{c}\)
Reference/center point on the RIS for local approximation.

          
\(\mathbf{r},\hat{\mathbf{r}}_n,\hat{\mathbf{r}}_i\)
Receiver-related vector and unit direction vectors in Sionna RIS equations.

          
\(\theta_r^\*\)
Effective reradiation angle in the Vitucci-based coverage-map model.

          
\(\alpha_r\)
Angular taper exponent in the coverage-map model.

          
\(j\)
Imaginary unit.

          
\(e\)
Base of natural exponential in phasor terms.

          
\(\pi\)
Pi constant.
